About Myself

I was born and brought up in Dhaka, Bangladesh. I have taken my IGCSE(O’Level) and IAL(A’Level) exam from Edexcel under British Council. I am currently a final year student of Computer Science and Engineering at Brac University. I am highly interested in Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Neural Network and wish to develop a career in these fields. Currently , I am doing my thesis in Networking and Machine Learning. I am also working in another Research paper which I expect to publish soon. In the last 3 years ,I have participated in many national and International Robotics competitions which has boosted my skills as a Robotics and Software Engineer.I have also participated in the first Robotics Reality show of Bangladesh – “GPH ISPAT ESHO ROBOT BANAI” which was broadcast in Channel I. We were the semifinalist of that competition and competed with private and public universities all over the country.
