Professional Experience

  • I have been working in a number of projects and competitions for the past 4 years in Brac University which are mostly funded by the ICT division and Brac University.The most notable one is “BRACU MONGOL TORI“. This is an Astronaut Assistant Robot capable of moving through the rough terrain of mars and is able to autonomously carry out some more tasks.I worked here as a mechanical and software Engineer.As a mechanical Engineer , I helped the team in making mechanical structures like arms , wheels and overall structure. Later, I worked in the Software and Control Team of Bracu Mongol Tori and made a Graphical User Interface using Python for the control and locomotion of the rover. I also worked in Image Processing tasks, like – Ball detection, Aruco Tag detection, etc. In 2020, we went to Vellore Institute of Technology, India to participate in Indian Rover Challenge and became 7th among teams from India, Bangladesh and Poland. In 2020, Bracu Mongol Tori was also ranked 3rd in the world in University Rover Challenge, which is a mars rover based internation competition in USA, according to Priliminary Design Review and System acceptancy Review scores. Recently, I have also became the Team Lead of Artificial Intelligence at Bracu MONGOL TORI with the aim to build a fully intelligent robot.  

Currently , I am working as a Research and Development Lead of a Startup named – “The Tech Lab“.It aims teach Technology and Robotics to people of all ages and also wants to start a Robotics and AI industry in Bangladesh.

I am also a Data Science Enthusiast and have done quite a few courses on Coursera, DataCamp and Kaggle.
